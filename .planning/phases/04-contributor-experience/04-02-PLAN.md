| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-04-05 | D | grouped update policy | mitigate | Group only `minor` and `patch` updates, keep the weekly cadence explicit, and cap open PRs at `5` so routine update noise does not overwhelm the maintainer workflow. |
| T-04-06 | R | CONTR-04 completion claim | mitigate | Separate the human merge step from the automated PR query, require proof that the config is on `main`, and only accept the phase after observing a real `dependabot/` PR targeting `main`. |
</threat_model>
